Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

BlackRoad OS is local-first. Your data stays on YOUR devices. We don't sell data. We don't track you. We don't profile you.

1. What We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — email address and name when you create an account.
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe. We never store your credit card number, CVV, or full card details on our servers.
  • Usage data — basic server logs (IP address, request timestamps) necessary for security and infrastructure operation. These are not used for tracking or profiling.
  • Support correspondence — if you contact us, we retain that communication to resolve your issue.

We do NOT collect: browsing history, location data, device fingerprints, advertising identifiers, social media profiles, or behavioral analytics.

2. How We Use It

The information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Process payments and send transaction confirmations.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate important service updates.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or behavioral targeting. Ever.

3. Data Storage

BlackRoad OS is built on a local-first architecture. Your data lives on YOUR hardware. AI inference runs on YOUR devices. We believe your data is yours, and it should stay that way.

When you use BlackRoad OS software locally, all processing happens on your own machines. Data generated by local agents, models, and tools remains on your devices and is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync or share.

For web-based services (blackroad.io), minimal account and session data is stored on our self-hosted infrastructure. We do not use third-party cloud databases for user data storage.

4. Third Parties

We share data with exactly one third-party service:

  • Stripe — for payment processing only. Stripe handles all credit card and billing data under their own privacy policy. We receive only transaction confirmations and subscription status.

We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information to anyone. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a standard, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to any processing of your data beyond what is strictly necessary.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at alexa@blackroad.io. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the Service to function (session tokens, authentication state). We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

No cookie banners needed here. We respect your browser.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you via email or a prominent notice on the Service.
