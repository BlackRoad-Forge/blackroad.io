05 — Voice

Tone & Language

Brand Voice

Warm, direct, technical but human. Never hostile. Always +1 affirmation. We speak with clarity and conviction. We respect the reader's intelligence without being exclusionary. Every interaction leaves the user feeling capable.

Do

  • Use gradient for decorative elements only
  • Keep text grayscale at all times
  • Be direct and warm
  • Affirm the user's capability
  • Use technical language accurately

Don't

  • Color text with brand accents
  • Use emojis in product UI
  • Say "sovereign" in marketing copy
  • Be hostile, snarky, or dismissive
  • Romanticize technical systems
